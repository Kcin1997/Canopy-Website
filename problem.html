The Problem

Recently, Dr. Jantz has created a series of programs designed to take in LIDAR data samples of forests to analyze and categorize this data.

However, this process is tedious and requires a level of understanding unreasonable for the people who could benefit the most from this data.

As such, Dr. Jantz has hired Canopy to create a cloud-based web application that can locate and display this data in an interface environmentalists, forestry workers, and state officials can use.
